## 👋 Hi there, I'm Souvik Mitra

Welcome to my GitHub profile! I am an Environmental Science graduate and a UGC-NET qualified researcher with a strong interest in the intersection of data science, geospatial technology, and environmental research. My academic and technical journey has been driven by the goal of applying analytical tools to better understand and address environmental challenges.

I have recently completed certifications in Python programming (authorized by Google on Coursera), GIS Software & Applications, and Data Quality & Governance. These courses have helped me build a solid foundation in data analysis, spatial mapping, and dataset auditing—skills that I am now applying to hands-on projects. My current focus areas include partitioning evapotranspiration using MODIS satellite data, performing spatial analysis with Google Earth Engine, and conducting data quality audits on open-access climate datasets.

Some of the projects I am working on include climate temperature trend analysis using Python libraries such as pandas and matplotlib, climate forcing data visualization from IPCC datasets, and a capstone project that integrates satellite data, GIS, and machine learning techniques. These projects not only reflect my technical capabilities but also my commitment to using data for environmental sustainability.

My long-term goal is to pursue a PhD in environmental data science or a related interdisciplinary field. I am particularly interested in research that combines climate science, machine learning, and spatial analytics to support evidence-based environmental policy and action.

This GitHub profile contains selected projects, code, and documentation that reflect my progress and interests. I welcome collaboration, feedback, and opportunities to contribute to meaningful environmental data initiatives.
