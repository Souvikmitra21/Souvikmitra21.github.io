This paper introduces a machine learning-based fault detection system for solar PV systems connected to the electrical grid. By analyzing patterns in voltage, current, and power output data, the models effectively classify electrical fault conditions. The system aids in early fault diagnosis, improving energy reliability and optimizing maintenance scheduling in renewable energy deployments.
