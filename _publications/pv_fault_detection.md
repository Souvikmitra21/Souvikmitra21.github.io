This paper proposes a machine learning-based framework for fault detection in photovoltaic (PV) systems integrated into the grid. A random forest algorithm is utilized to classify electrical faults while convolutional neural networks handle environmental and shading-related external faults. The proposed approach improves reliability and operational safety in solar energy infrastructure.
