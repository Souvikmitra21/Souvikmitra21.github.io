### Environmental Engineering intern | (June 2023 - July 2023)

_Bharat Coking Coal Limited, Dhanbad, India_

- Conducted comprehensive assessments of eco parks and various mining sites within Cluster 4 groups, including Salanpur, Katras Choitudih, Amalgamated West Mudidih-Keshalpur, Amalgamated Angarpathra-Ramkanali, and Gaslitand.
- Utilized advanced equipment such as Respirable Dust Sampler and High Volume Air Sampler to meticulously monitor air quality, ensuring adherence to regulatory standards.
- Analyzed key parameters including Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD), sulfate concentration, oil and grease concentration, and NOX concentration to assess and enhance water quality.
- Evaluated multiple environmental factors such as noise levels, blasting vibration, socio-economic dynamics, flora & fauna distribution, land resources, traffic patterns, and visual aesthetics to identify potential impacts and propose effective mitigation measures.
- Collaborated with multidisciplinary teams to develop and implement strategies aimed at minimizing adverse environmental effects while maximizing operational efficiency.
- Demonstrated strong analytical skills and attention to detail in generating comprehensive reports and recommendations for sustainable environmental management.
