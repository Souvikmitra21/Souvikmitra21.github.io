### Book Chapter

### Conference Proceedings

**Mitra, S.** and **Chinmaya, K. A.** (2025). *Fault Detection in PV Grid Integrated System via Machine Learning Technology.*  
In *2025 IEEE 1st International Conference on Smart and Sustainable Developments in Electrical Engineering (SSDEE)*, pp. 1–6.  
[DOI: 10.1109/SSDEE64538.2025.10967651](https://doi.org/10.1109/SSDEE64538.2025.10967651)

This study proposes a machine learning-based fault detection framework for photovoltaic (PV) systems integrated with the electrical grid. Using Random Forests and Logistic Regression, the model identifies electrical faults under dynamic operating conditions, supporting predictive maintenance and performance optimization in solar infrastructure.
