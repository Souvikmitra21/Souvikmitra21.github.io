Welcome to my corner of the digital realm where the intersection of environmental science and coding takes center stage. I am Souvik, an Environmental Scientist with a fervent passion for coding and its potential to drive positive change in the world.

My academic journey includes a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, with ongoing pursuit of a Master's degree in Environmental Science and Technology. Alongside my academic endeavors, I have delved into the realms of programming, with proficiency in Java and an ongoing exploration of Python.

My practical experience includes enriching stints as an Environmental Engineering Intern at the esteemed Bharat Coking Coal Limited (Coal India) and as an intern at the Krishi Vigyan Kendra. These experiences have not only honed my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of the intricate relationship between environmental dynamics and technological solutions.

I am driven by a relentless curiosity to explore innovative ways in which technology can be leveraged to address pressing environmental challenges. Through my work, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the preservation and sustainability of our planet.

Let's collaborate and code a greener, more sustainable future together.
