### Analysed the various Parameters of Soil | Krishi Vigyan Kendra(september 2022 – Sept 2022)

- pH Assessment: Employed standardized methodologies to determine the pH levels of soil samples collected from distinct fields. pH values serve as a fundamental indicator of soil acidity or alkalinity, crucial for optimizing crop growth and nutrient availability.

Electrical Conductivity Measurement: Utilized precise techniques to gauge the electrical conductivity of soil samples, providing insights into soil salinity and its potential impact on crop health and water management strategies.

Organic Carbon Estimation: Conducted thorough analysis to quantify organic carbon content within soil samples, a vital component influencing soil fertility, structure, and overall agricultural sustainability.

Nutrient Evaluation: Methodically analyzed soil samples to ascertain levels of essential nutrients such as nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and sulphur. These nutrients play pivotal roles in supporting plant growth, yield, and overall crop health.
